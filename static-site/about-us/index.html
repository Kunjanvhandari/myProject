OUR MISSION

Empowering Minds Through Knowledge

At SaraswatiLibrary, our mission is to democratize access to knowledge and foster a culture of lifelong learning. We believe that every individual deserves the opportunity to explore, learn, and grow regardless of their background or circumstances.

We are committed to curating a diverse collection of books, digital resources, and educational materials that cater to students, researchers, educators, and casual readers alike. Through innovative technology and dedicated service, we aim to bridge the gap between curiosity and knowledge.

OUR VISION

Building a Knowledge-Powered Society

We envision a world where knowledge is not a privilege but a fundamental right. Our vision is to create a vibrant ecosystem where readers, authors, and communities come together to celebrate the power of ideas and the written word.

Looking ahead, we aim to expand our digital infrastructure, establish satellite reading centers in underserved communities, and pioneer AI-powered personalized reading recommendations. We want to make SaraswatiLibrary the most accessible and innovative library in Nepal.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones That Define Us

A look back at the key moments that shaped SaraswatiLibrary into what it is today.

2020

Founded

SaraswatiLibrary was established with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a space where knowledge is freely accessible to all. Starting with a modest collection of 5,000 books and a team of 5 passionate individuals, we opened our doors to the community.

2021

Digital Launch

We launched our digital platform, bringing over 50,000 e-books and online resources to readers across Nepal. This milestone allowed us to reach remote areas and provide 24/7 access to our growing collection of digital content.

2022

Community Hub

We expanded our services to become a full-fledged community hub, hosting book clubs, author talks, workshops, and children's reading programs. Our membership grew to over 10,000 active readers, and we introduced collaborative study spaces.

2023

Expansion

We opened two new branches and partnered with schools and universities across the country. Our collection surpassed 50,000 physical books, and we were recognized with the National Library Innovation Award for our contributions to education and technology.
